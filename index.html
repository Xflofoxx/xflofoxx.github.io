Programming is art.

When you see the empty file before your eyes, you feel the same feeling that test a painter in front of a canvas or a sculptor, or a craftsman in front of source material.

When you face a problem you think the the solution and in your mind are formed myriad ways to reach that goal and the choices you make are the shape of your art.

When you write a line of code your mind explores the streets of the subsequent and the possible implications of your choice. That makes you an insatiable artist, often unhappy with your own work, always hungry...
